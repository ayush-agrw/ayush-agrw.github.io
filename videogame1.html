Environments

The snow environment includes a Triplanar Shader for every asset which I created myself in Unreal Engine 5 as well. It calculates the angle and position at which an object is placed relative to the world axes, and then places snow automatically on the mesh such that it points upwards and is always on top, shifting dynamically no matter how its parent mesh is moved or rotated.
